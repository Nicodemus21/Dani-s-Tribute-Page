Mama Eucabeth

The woman who lived for others
Danny in the middle, spending a Sabbath evening with her family.

    Here is tentative timeline of MAMA EUCA'S life:

  • 1938 -Born in Karachuonyo to Mzee Micah Olonde.
  • 1945 -She commences her education at Rachuonyo Primary School.
  • 1952 -She finishes her O-level education and starts working a Kendu Bay SDA Press Publishers and met and fell in love with her devoted husband the late Pastor Caleb Owuor.
  • 1953 -She gets married to Pastor Caleb Owuor and she conceives the first born of her womb Mwalimu Benson Owuor.
  • 1955 -Being passionate about education, she begins Winyo Primary School school teaching adult education to the women in the village.
  • 1960 -Haunted by the high infant mortality rates in the village, she begins midwifing and teaching the villagers on proper ante and postnatal care.
  • 2018 -She dies within three months after suffering a broken femur.

"Mama Euca's life and achievements are a testimony on how far we all need unshakable faith in the Lord. She sufferred a great deal of tribulation while living but she forever clinged to her faith, for she surrendered her life to God to let it be according to his will. Indeed she did run her race with graceful endurance to the end. We hope to meet forever at the FIRST Ressurection"

---Paul Owino Obange Ooko, Grandson
